Please read the below rules before making any Tee Time requests
  • Please communicate with your group before making a request - multiple requests made from the same group will be ignored in the lottery
  • Guests are not permitted
  • You are responsible for any fees associated with your Tee Time
  • Abuse of the reciprocal program including no-shows, bringing guests or general disrespect may result in loss of future Tee Times
  • Members will be limited to 2 Requests per day
  • Tee Time requests must be for a minimum of two players - twosomes will be matched up to maximize avilable tee times
  • Please refer to the home clubs website for their Dress Code and any other Policies
Thank You!
