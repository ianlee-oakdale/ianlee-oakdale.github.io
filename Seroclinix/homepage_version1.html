Innovative diagnostic solutions for animal health, wellness and reproduction
Seroclinix focus is on the development of specialized diagnostic testing and services for veterinarians, farmers, and breeders. Our core business initiative is to provide easy-to-use, fast, accurate testing solutions using the latest technology to improve animal health, wellness and reproduction. Our portfolio of tests and lab services are available for companion animals and livestock.
